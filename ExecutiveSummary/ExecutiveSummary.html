# Execute Summary

Lucky is an provably fair gaming platform that runs on the Ethereum Network. We aim to revolutionize the gaming industry through harnessing blockchain technology to provide a provably fair gaming mechanism in the online gaming market.

The LUCK Network token is a modular utility token that fuels the Lucky Gaming Platform. This includes the LUCK gaming on the Lucky Gaming Platform, Governance of the Lucky Gaming Platform and Computational staking for provably fair gaming.

This whitepaper will give an overview of how we are delivering our proposed solution.

# An Experienced Team you can trust

Our executive team have over 40 years of experience in the online gaming industry and has collectively overseen more than six million player signups, billions of dollars in player bets and has published games that have sold millions of units at retail.

# The Platform

We are excited to announce the Lucky Gaming Platform. Our Ethereum-based technology includes the following:

  • Provably fair gaming mechanism
  • Ability for players to stake token to become the house
  • Instant convertibility to any crypto assets
  • is cheaper than other blockchain based gaming platform
  • have better odds than other blockchain based gaming platform
  • better affiliates reward program
  • will be protected by several technology patents (pending)

# LUCK Token in Brief

Our token LUCK will be used for all platform actions, including betting, lending, paying out players, incentivizing affiliates and compensating stakeholders. The total supply of LUCK tokens will be 10 billion tokens for the 1st 3 year, after which there will be a yearly emission of 100 million tokens which is an annual inflation of 1% and decreasing. LUCK tokens will be created during a single event commencing [ June 22, 2019 at 14:00 UTC ]. It will be released over 3 years to the LUCKY ecosystem. Token allocation details are listed under section 7 below. To keep inline with the cyberpunk spirit, we will be releasing over 60% of the LUCK token through ecosystem incentives ( transactional mining, developer incentives & affiliate referrals ) to the community. The remaining tokens will be used for platform operations, team salary, marketing of the platform and platform gaming capital. This will ensure smooth operations over the next 3 years until the self-governance DAO kicks in.
