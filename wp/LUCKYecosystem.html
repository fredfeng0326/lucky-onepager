# LUCKY ecosystem

For the decentralised Lucky ecosystem to self sustain as a DAO, it requires different actors to fulfill different functionality within the system:

  • Players: Users of the platform
  • Lucky Platform: Gaming destination governed by community
  • Developers: Create new games on the platform
  • Affiliates: Helps the platform with marketing and attract new users
  • Stakers: Users whom help govern the platform while receiving incentives for their contribution

# Players

Without players, there will be no ecosystem. Thus players are the most essential actor within the Lucky platform. We believe that players value online gaming sites that are fair and transparent while providing good odds and user experience.

Thus we prioritize these features to ensure that it has the best environment to keep players engaged.

# Platform

Lucky Platform is designed to function across two phases of development. Specifically as we transit from a centralized governance model to a decentralised governance model where the community takes over the operational decisions of the platform.

In the decentralized governance model, decisions are decided through a voting process. The following are some of the decisions that may be voted on:

  • Games to be published
  • Game publishing fees
  • Game revenue sharing rates
  • Decision making team
  • Game odds
  • Uses of governance fund to pay for platform operations
  • Affiliate reward rates upon end of transactional mining
  • Player incentive rates upon end of transactional mining
  • Token supply and token burn scenarios

# Developers

We aspire to make Lucky a long term success thus we cater a separate allocation of tokens to incentivize developers to create the best games on the Lucky platform.

Game developers would be vetted by the governance team for approvals to publish on the Lucky Platform. Upon approval of the games, the developers would receive the base publishing fee and subsequently a share of the gaming fee and profit when players play their game.

Revenue would be calculated and distributed at the end of each month.

# Affiliates

Affiliate referrals serves as the social marketing function for the Lucky platform. This is especially important during the early days of the platform to attract users to the site.

# Stakers

Stakers serve two functions; being the provider of decentralised gaming capital and being the entity responsible for amending governance specifics of the platform.

Through providing gaming capital, stakers take a cut of the profit from the platform based on the amount of LUCK token they staked. Profit would be determined probabilistically based on gaming odds of the game played. In the long run, the odds will always be in favor of the house and stakers can share in the profit that the Lucky platform gets.

Since stakers have vested interest in the platform, it creates the right incentives for them to vote through the governance process and amend details of the platform. This ensure the long term viability of both the LUCK token and the LUCKY platform.
