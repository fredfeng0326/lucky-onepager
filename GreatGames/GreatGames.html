# Greate Games - Bases on Experience

Combined, Lucky’s founders have a proven track record of over 40 years’ creating innovative interactive entertainment products and have sold millions of games in the process. We know what it takes to make a good casino game great and now we’ve built the world’s first blockchain-based platform that is capable of meeting the demands of a high-quality online gaming experience.

Our core team comprises more than ten full-time developers and we have already completed the first blockchain-based 3D games using the Lucky Gaming Platform in HTML5. These initial games stand as a fully-playable proof of concept, demonstrating the advanced capabilities of Lucky’s platform and serving as inspiration in order to kickstart the ecosystem of operators and developers around the world.

Games built on the Lucky Gaming Platform run directly in the browser on desktop and mobile devices without requiring an app download, completely eliminating one of the major pain-points of player onboarding.
